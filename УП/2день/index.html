Hi! I'm Tom. I am a third-year student and studying to be a web developer

Being a web developer is not only writing where, like some kind of programmer, but also being a designer. You need to be able to choose colors that are combined, the ability to make a beautiful design and logo "with your own hands".

The main "disadvantages" of the profession include:
– the work of web programmers is sedentary, their work is fraught with pain in the back, wrists, lower back, posture may deteriorate. Vision problems may occur;
– in the process of work, programmers interact with all team members, they do not always manage to reach a compromise. There may be difficulties with the customer, who is often far away and unable to clearly express his desires;
– irregular working hours. If it is urgently necessary to finalize the project, the programmer can spend days working on the code;
– representatives of this profession need to constantly adapt to certain new trends in information technology, engage in self-education.

Obvious advantages of the profession:
– the demand for web programmers in the labor market. Every self-respecting firm or company has its own website that needs to be managed;
– a web programmer has a high salary. As a rule, very significant salaries, sometimes even exceeding the earnings of the director of their company;
– self-study opportunities. Web programmers do not need official diplomas. Knowledge is important, and the necessary knowledge can be found on the Internet.
